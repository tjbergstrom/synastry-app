About

This is just a fun way to see the synastry match between birthdays. How compatible are you?

You can try out Match Two Birthdays by inputting your birthday and someone else's, and see how compatible you are.

If you already know your full chart, you can try Match Two Charts and manually enter each planet from each chart. You can reference a more detailed birth chart at Alabe.

You can also use the birth chart calculator to see your or someone else's birthchart. I made my own calculator based on as much data as I could find, and it might not be extremely accurate. It's the same calculator used by the "Match Two Birthdays" synastry calculator.
